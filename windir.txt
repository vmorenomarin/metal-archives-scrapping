<div class="band_comment clear">
<!-- Max 400 characters. Open the rest in a dialogue box-->
	    		    	"Windir" is an ancient Norse word, translatable as "warrior".<br/>
<br/>
Windir performed their last concert in Oslo on September 3rd 2004 in honor of <a href="https://www.metal-archives.com/artists/Valfar/5436">Valfar</a>. <a href="https://www.metal-archives.com/bands/Enslaved/104">Enslaved</a>, <a href="https://www.metal-archives.com/bands/Finntroll/95">Finntroll</a> and <a href="https://www.metal-archives.com/bands/Mindgrinder/5275">Mindgrinder</a> were the guest bands. <br/>
<br/>
The remaining members formed <a href="https://www.metal-archives.com/bands/Vreid/27072">Vreid</a>.
